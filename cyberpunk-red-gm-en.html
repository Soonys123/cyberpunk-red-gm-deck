CYBERPSYCHOSIS
▶
Humanity (HUM) measures a character's mental balance. Each cyberware implant erodes some of it.
CONCEPTVALUE / RULE
Maximum HumanityEMP × 10
Effective EMPCurrent HUM ÷ 10 (round down) — replaces EMP on rolls
Loss on installationRoll the dice indicated by the implant (e.g., 2d6 HUM lost)
Cyberpsychosis triggeredHUM drops to 0 or below
PRE-CRISIS SYMPTOMS (low HUM):
At 20 HUM or below — the GM secretly rolls 1d10 after each violent or stressful act. If the result is less than or equal to the number of tens lost, a symptom manifests (paranoia, dissociation, unprovoked violence, flat affect…).

CRISE DE CYBERPSYCHOSIS (HUM = 0) :
The character is taken over by the GM. They become a threat to everyone — including allies. The only solution is neutralization (taken out of combat) followed by intensive therapy.
HUMANITY RECOVERYGAIN
Therapy (1 week, Medtech or therapist)+1d6 HUM
Removal of a cyberware implantRecovers half of initial loss
Rest and stable social life+1 HUM/week (GM)
⚡ Some implants cause no loss (basic cyberoptics, neural ports…). "Cyborg" implants (full limb replacement) cause greater losses. Cyberpsychosis is irreversible without treatment — the character becomes an NPC.
Skill roll: STAT + Skill + 1d10 vs DV set by the GM.
Unmastered skills: roll with STAT only + 1d10 (no skill bonus).
